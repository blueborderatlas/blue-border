This is the starting note for a coastal month in Egypt, from June 1 to June 30, 2026. It will stay unfinished on purpose for now, because the point is to let the trip change the page while it happens.

The route begins with the Red Sea in mind: Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh, warm water, dry light, long hotel edges and the particular calm that comes from looking at a sea that feels almost unreal in color.

But Egypt is not only the Red Sea. Cairo belongs in the month too, even without a beach, because every coastal trip needs a city interruption. Later, Marsa Matruh pulls the route north toward the Mediterranean, a different blue, a different wind, a different idea of summer.

Updates will continue during the trip. For now, this page is a marker: water temperature, walking notes, stays, transport, coffee stops, and the small moments that decide whether a place becomes more than a name on the route.
